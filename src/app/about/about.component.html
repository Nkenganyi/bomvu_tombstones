About Bomvu Tombstones

Bomvu Tombstone Manufacturers is the largest granite producer with a state-of-the-art cutting and polishing factory using the best technology, situated in the Eastern Cape!!

We specialize in uniquely designed tombstones and monuments, supplying clients with high quality memorials at very affordable prices — while offering a wide range that fits your needs and your budget.

With BT, choosing a headstone for your loved one is easy with the assistance of our friendly and professional staff.

Each design is available in 3 variants:
  • Headstone with base
  • Headstone, base and kerbs
  • Full body tombstone (headstone, base, kerbs and ledger)

We also provide vinyl and sandblasting. Talk to our friendly and professional staff who are ready to listen and assist.
